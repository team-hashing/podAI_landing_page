Our Story

We are two 23-year-old Computer Science graduates from Spain, bound by our shared passion for technology and a relentless drive to make a meaningful impact. Our journey began in the classrooms and labs of our university, where our paths first crossed during our degree. Little did we know that this meeting would spark the beginning of a partnership that would lead us to create something truly extraordinary.

Together, we founded Team Hashing, a company born out of our shared vision to innovate and bring our tech ideas to life. From the start, our mission has been clear: to harness the power of technology to solve real-world problems and create experiences that inspire.

Our journey hasn't been without its challenges, but each obstacle has only fueled our determination. In 2022, our dedication paid off when we entered and won the IKEA Hackathon. Competing against some of the brightest minds in the industry, our project stood out for its creativity, technical prowess, and practical application.

Not content with just one victory, we continued to push the boundaries of what's possible. In 2024, we once again found ourselves in the winner's circle—this time at the Google Hackathon. This achievement not only solidified our reputation as innovators but also affirmed our belief that with hard work, collaboration, and a little bit of boldness, we can achieve anything we set our minds to.

Our journey has been a whirlwind of learning, growth, and incredible experiences. We've faced tough challenges, celebrated big wins, and most importantly, we've remained true to our core values every step of the way. As we continue to forge ahead, we're excited to share our story with you and to bring even more groundbreaking ideas to life.

Thank you for being a part of our journey—this is just the beginning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way content is created and consumed through the power of artificial intelligence. With our AI podcast generator, we're breaking new ground by allowing anyone to effortlessly generate high-quality podcast content on any topic, anytime, anywhere. This tool represents our first step towards a future where AI plays a central role in content creation, democratizing the process and making it more accessible, efficient, and personalized for everyone.

We believe in the transformative potential of AI to reshape the media landscape, empowering creators and listeners alike. By harnessing advanced technology, we aim to eliminate barriers to content creation, enabling more voices to be heard and more stories to be shared. Our vision is to create a world where AI not only assists but enhances human creativity, opening up new possibilities for storytelling and communication.

This project is just the beginning. As we continue to innovate, our goal remains clear: to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI, bringing the future of content creation to the present.

Our Values

Our company is built on a foundation of strong values that guide every decision we make and every project we undertake. At the heart of everything we do is a deep commitment to privacy, innovation, and making a positive impact.

Privacy is not just a feature—it's a fundamental principle. We believe that technology should empower users without compromising their privacy or security. That’s why we prioritize protecting our users' data, ensuring that our innovations are both cutting-edge and respectful of individual rights.

Innovation drives us to constantly push the boundaries of what’s possible. We are passionate about exploring new ideas, experimenting with emerging technologies, and developing solutions that not only meet the needs of today but anticipate the challenges of tomorrow. Our goal is to create tools that are not only powerful but also accessible, enabling everyone to benefit from technological advancements.

These values are more than just words—they are the principles that define us. As we continue to grow and innovate, we remain steadfast in our commitment to privacy, innovation, and making a meaningful impact, all while upholding the highest ethical standards.
